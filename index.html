How to play
  SHOW

Controls

  • -- [ENTER] to select

  • -- [RIGHT ARROW] to move shooter right

  • -- [LEFT ARROW] to move shooter left

  • -- [SPACE] to shoot

Instructions

  • -- Shoot the enemies coming down from the top

  • -- Watch your ammo (destroying 1 enemy = +1 ammo)

  • -- If enemies reach the bottom -1 life (when no lives are left, game over)

  • -- Speed of enemies increases over time

  • -- Random events will help you - speed reset, double laser, triple laser
